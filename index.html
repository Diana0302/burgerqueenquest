Burgers, are more than just two buns and a patty. The composition of food is more than just a mere task, a cook must learn to surprise the customer with taste that flow.

Burgers In LA

Ruby's Dinner in Citadel. The classic cheeseburger. The meat and cheese can be to over powering to the point where your mouth may feel like it needs more lettuce. 3.5/5

BJ's in Montebello Mall. Just a quick btw: these are not all my pictures, just because not all of us like to be wierdos in front of stragers. Anyways Bacon Burger not the best Idea for BJ's I can not taste anything else but the bacon. 2/5

Chilli's on Pico Rivera on Washington. The Classic Bacon burger on the dinner for 2. I love the idea that picles are put on the side because as I was eating my burger and the meat became a nuisance a bite into a pickle reminded me of the heaven a burger is. 4/5

The Black Bear Diner. Located in near San Fransisco. Just a heads up this buger was the size of my face, Bob's Big Bear Buger. I will just say this burger was the definition of a try hard, its efforts were successful. A great flow between the fresh veggies and the juicy meat. The buns are warm and a little toasty great for crunchy lettuce and no soggy bread. 4/5

La Cabana. Located in East LA. This place just has amazing burgers, something about the fact that the burger isn't overflowing with too much chese or lettuce makes it the perfect combination. Your mouth will want more of its heaven simplicity. 5/5
